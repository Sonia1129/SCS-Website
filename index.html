About Us

SCS is Neemrana based crane service which provides all types of crane like Farana (F-15), Boom-Lift, Fork-Lift, Tower Crane and Hydra Crane.

For a long time, we have engaged ourselves in the service of Crane hiring and selling and various other services related to it. Our customers know us for our best service. And hence we are the topmost choice of the customers once attached to us. We provide a miscellaneous and contemporary fleet. The cranes at us are ensured complete users' safety and best quality so that the user can carry out his work hassle-free. When it is about our customer’s satisfaction we don’t compromise anything. We endeavor our finest to offer you the best that we can.

We provide the cranes to various sectors like private and public for carrying out the projects related to power plants, cement plants, refineries, construction of roads and bridges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers everywhere with premium services and solutions for lifting, installing, and decommissioning large and heavy structures in any project and to improve their construction efficiency and optimize the uptime of their plants and operations.

Our Plan

We can plan and manage all your lifting requirements from the initial site survey. Included in our Contract Hire:

  • Site Survey
  • Crane Supervisor
  • Crane and Operators
  • Lifting Equipment and Accessories

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most customer-centric company for crane service in the world by providing outstanding quality, service, and value to our customers.
